AI Literacy for Educators

This study conducts a systematic literature review with the aim of providing holistic guidance on enhancing AI literacy for educators. The whole review process is guided by a designed five-dimension framework: educators’ AI literacy definition dimensions, understanding AI core knowledge, practice AI in educational settings, perspectives of AI in education, and AI pedagogies in education, informed by Bloom’s Taxonomy. Particularly, we investigated the commonly discussed dimensions of AI literacy for educators and emphasized the importance of adding the assessment dimension to enhance educators’ AI literacy effectively. For improving educators’ AI core knowledge relevant to AI literacy, we proposed a layered structure with detailed descriptions of the key skills, pedagogical strategies, evaluation approaches and expected learning outcomes to guide educators in building their AI competencies progressively. Then, we systematically discussed common AI applications in education, approaches to enhance educators’ perceptions of AI in education and effective AI pedagogies to engage with AI in educational practices. Overall, the implementation of this literature review provides detailed, step-by-step guidance for initiating professional development or training programs dealing with educators’ AI literacy.
