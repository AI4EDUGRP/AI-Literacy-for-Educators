AI Literacy for Educators

Opportunities

Researchers are optimistic about the capabilities of AI in streamlining administrative tasks and supporting personalized learning instructions. Moreover, they also recognized that application of AI in educational settings will facilitate educators to provide personalized and timely feedback (Meng et al., 2022), create inclusive learning materials (Zhang & Zhang, 2024), enhance educational strategies (Ayanwale et al., 2024), manage classroom activities, and deal with stress and workload (Hashem et al., 2024). Similarly, for students, the integration of AI has the potential to support self-paced learning (Meng et al., 2022), refine their writing and research questions (Pretorius, 2023), support cognitive engagement (Wang & Lu, 2023), and foster creativity, critical thinking and higher-order thinking skills (ElSayary, 2023).
