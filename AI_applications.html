The application settings and associated educational practices

In the retrieved literature, researchers discussed various AI applications in diverse settings including: Teacher Education (Olari & Romeike, 2021; Meng et al., 2022; Wang & Lu, 2023; van den Berg & du Plessis, 2023; Black et al., 2024; Du et al., 2024; Zhang & Zhang, 2024), PK-12 and Higher Education (Henry et al., 2021; Hays et al., 2024; Lérias et al., 2024; Ruiz et al., 2024), General Learning Environments (Wang et al., 2023; Koh et al., 2023; Wang, 2023; Hashem et al., 2024, etc.), and other specific area such as Computer Science Education (Olari et al., 2024) and Technology Education (Stolpe & Hallström, 2024). These applications involve using AI to support student learning activities, which include promoting personalized learning, cultivating critical thinking skills, facilitating academic writing; and facilitating teaching practices, which include supporting automated grading, creating content and assessments, maintaining interactive learning environments and so on.
