Dimensions of AI literacy for educators discussed in the prior literature

As shown in this figure, prior studies were first grouped together according to the definition dimensions they mentioned. Then, within each dimension, more fine-grained AI knowledge and relevant considerations were extracted and coded. Particularly, in the figure, the sizes of the bars indicate the frequencies of the corresponding dimensions discussed in the retrieved papers. In each dimension, it can be found that there is a considerable variation in terms of both categories and times of the discussed AI knowledge. Take the analysis related to Fundamental and Technical Knowledge as an example, researchers mentioned 10 different categories of AI knowledge with various mentioned times. Among these categories, several instances of overlap were observed, like Machine Learning Algorithms and Supervised Learning, indicating a lack of unified structure in organizing AI knowledge.
